What is STRATEGEM?

Strategem is an advanced platform designed to help League of Legends players elevate their gameplay with powerful AI-driven insights, strategic advice, and up-to-date meta analysis.

Combining an intelligent AI chatbot with comprehensive tools like tier lists, champion selection guides, and gameplay strategies, Strategem offers personalized recommendations that help players make smarter decisions, improve their skills and climb the ranks faster.

Why use Strategem?

Our team created this site with a single goal in mind: to make high-level strategic insights accessible to every League of Legends player. The game is constantly evolving, and staying on top of changes in the meta, champion performance, and gameplay strategies can be overwhelming.

Have you ever had a hard time catching up with the current meta when you've just returned playing? Or you're a hardcore player wanting to climb the ranks faster. That's where Strategem comes in.
